Utopia Craft v0.9

The modpack for the people. It's an FPS, it's a magical RPG, it's also a space race simulator with unlimited planetary exploration.

Utopia Craft uses Real Terrain Generation. Immerse yourself in picture perfect terrain.

Since this mod uses it's own standalone launcher, client side mods such as better foliage and recommended shaders are preconfigured and ready to go. It just works.
