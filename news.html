Utopia v0.9

Utopia is modpack for the people. Behold FPS, magical RPG, space race simulator with planet exploration, furniture, kitchen and cooking.

Utopia overrides standard Minecraft map generation with its own Real Terrain Generation.

This is a standalone launcher. You don't need Minecraft installed. The launcher installs client side mods to make the trees round and water and sky look like Unreal Engine.

If there is an error during launch download JDK 8 Java SE Development Kit 8u181

If you don't have at least a 1080 graphics card. Turn off the shaders.
